Dear Bobfathers,

This past year has been a pleasure. It was great seeing some of you at Kotobuki and at the Insomnia Cookie session at Bobst back in the fall. As such, CAS has been learned from us what worked and what didn’t, improving the program for the rising sophomore class and the incoming freshman. Whatever your experience, know that you have helped make the program better for the students in the lower grades.
